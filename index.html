Community Science museum

Fun with science

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we're committed to making science accessible to all

Science is meant to be fun. We have a burning passion to teach kids science; with interactive activities, historical artifacts, and theoretical science suitable for all ages.

Our attractions are always here waiting to be explored. As well as attractions, we also have weekly events, which provides further learning in a specific science category.
